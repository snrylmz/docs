## Team page structure

`/settings/team` contains two core tables:

- Active members
- Pending invitations

## Roles

- `owner`: full management authority
- `admin`: operational management authority
- `member`: usage-focused access

## Permission matrix (summary)

- Invite member: owner, admin
- Cancel invitation: owner, admin
- Change role: owner
- Remove member: role-restricted owner/admin actions

## Invitation flow

1. Open `Invite Member`
2. Enter email and role
3. Send invitation
4. Track status in pending invitations

## Invite acceptance

Invited users accept from tokenized invitation page.
Upon acceptance, membership is activated in that tenant.

## Role updates

Owner can change role for eligible members directly in table controls.

## Member removal

Eligible users can remove members through row actions.

## Team governance recommendations

- Maintain at least two owner/admin users per tenant
- Clean stale invitations weekly
- Remove former team members on the same day
